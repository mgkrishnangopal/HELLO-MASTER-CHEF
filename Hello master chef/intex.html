CHICKEN BIRYANI

Biryani is a popular rice dish made with aromatic indianspices with the meat or vegetables.Dish made with chicken named chicken biryani.

INCREDIENTS:(for 1kg biryani)

  • Basmati rice-1kg
  • chicken-1kg
  • oil-200ml
  • ginger garlic paste-required
  • tomato-400gm
  • cardamom-1gm
  • onion -2 nos
  • coriander leaves- half hand
  • red chilli powder-2tablespoon
  • cinnamon-1gm
  • clove-1gm
  • salt-2 tablespoon
  • bay leaf-2 nos
  • curd-200ml
  • lemon-1/2

HOW TO MAKE BRIYANI:

  1. Heat refined oil in a pan. once oil is hot enought add bay leaf,cardamom,cloves saute fof a minute .
  2. Then add onion and saute until pink.now added chicken.
  3. Also add ginger garlic paste,red chilli powder, coriander leaves. mix well all the spices and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Next add curd into it and give mix. turn flame to medium.
  5. Add water required.cook till the chicken is tender. then add 1kg of rice and spread evenly
  6. Finally add ghee or lemon what we want. cook without lid else cover it and give dum effect.
